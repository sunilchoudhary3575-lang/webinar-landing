The Modern Patient Journey

How Patients Choose a Clinic Today

Today's patients don't book an appointment after seeing just one ad. They research, compare, and build trust before making a decision. Here's the journey most patients follow:

Step 01

Search on Google

Patients search for their symptoms, treatments, or use phrases like "best clinic near me" on Google.

Step 02

Find Your Clinic

They see your clinic profile listed in local Google Maps search results and view your ranking.

Step 03

Read Reviews

They read Google reviews to see other patients' experiences, ratings, and testimonials to build trust.

Step 04

Learn About You

They visit your website or profile to check your specific qualifications, clinical experience, and doctor info.

Step 05

Check Your Social Media

They check your social media profiles (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube) to see educational videos and active community engagement.

Step 06

Contact Your Clinic

They call your front desk, drop an enquiry form, or reach out directly on WhatsApp to ask about availability.

Step 07

Book an Appointment

They finalize their booking with your coordinator and reserve a slot for their consultation.

Step 08

Visit, Review & Refer

They visit your clinic, have their consultation, leave a review, and refer friends or family members.
